About Meal Bridge

Meal Bridge is a platform designed to connect students with reliable local tiffin service providers..

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthy, homemade meals easily accessible to students near colleage while supporting local food vendors.

How we Work

Talk to vendor

Direct communication with vendors lets students ask questions, make requests, or discuss dietary needs.

Support Local

Meal Bridge helps local tiffin providers reach students in need of healthy, homemade meals..

Community

We are passionate about supporting local communities by sourcing from local farms and giving back to those in need.
